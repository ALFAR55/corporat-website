About Our Service


Healthy Food, Healthy Body!

Natural Eat is a partnership business which has been founded in 2021 in Bandar Sungai Petani, Kedah. Natural Eat consist of 5 founder and will be launch in 2022. Natural Eat is a catering business that serve food and drink at large public events and even smaller private ones. Natural Eat is a service that delivers food to clientâ€™s location, and we also cook and serve it on site if our clients demands so.

Our main service is to provide catering service to anyone who wants to hold an event, party, and celebration. Our service consists of setting up the tables, chairs, preparing food, clean-up dirty or discarded dishware, clean-up broken glasses, and politely accommodating the needs of all guests. we believe in our service and the foods we prepare can satisfy our customers since all of our foods are from organic sources, clean and guaranteed halal and nutritious to eat by anyone. We are also concerned with the health and feelings of our customers by asking about any foods they should not eat due to health or religious factors so we can exclude the menu from our services.
